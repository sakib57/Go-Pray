Salat ul Tahajjud

نَوَايْتُ اَنْ اُصَلِّىَ لِلَّّهِ تَعَالَى رَكْعَتَىْ صَلَوةِ التَّهَجُّدِ سُنَّةُ رَسُوْلِ اللَّهِ تَعَالَى مُتَوَجِّهًا اِلَى جِهَةِ الْكَعْبَةِ الشَّرِيْفَةِ اَللَّهُ اَكْبَرُ

Pronunciation: “Nawayatu Aan Usallia Lillahi Taalaa Rakatai Salaateet Tahajjudee Sunnatu Rasullahi Taala Mutaouuazzihan Ila Zihatil Ka’batish Sharifati, Allahuu Akbar”

Translation: (I Headed for ALLAH Intent to Pray Tahajjud’s 2 Rakat Rasul ALLAH Taala’s Sunnat Prayer Facing towards Kabah, ALLAH is Most Great)
